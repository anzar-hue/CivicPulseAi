A guided civic complaint assistant that helps citizens explain problems clearly, detect the right issue type, and understand where the complaint should go.

How CivicPulse AI Works

1. Describe the issue

Citizens can write complaints naturally instead of choosing rigid categories.

2. AI understands the complaint

The system detects multiple issues, urgency, ambiguity, and ownership.

3. Get guided output

The platform suggests the responsible authority and gives a clear next step.

Built for Real Civic Confusion

A single complaint like “road problem” can mean potholes, illegal construction, delayed work, or blocked public paths. CivicPulse AI is designed to handle that complexity.
